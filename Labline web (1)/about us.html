    About the Company.


    LABLINE INDUSTRIES (LI) is highly reputed organization dedicated to manufacturing Industrial valves of the highest quality. The company is manned by a dedicated workforce, both on and off the shop floor. A watchful Management and Quality Control department ensures that all processes are regulated and delivery schedules are closely adhered to. A constant level of stock is maintained in our standard product ranges in order to facilitate early deliveries and the company is approved by NTPC. We have also carried third party inspection from SGS, QUEST, TUV & BUREAU VERTIAS.



    LABLINE is known for its commitment to quality. Input materials are sourced from reliable and reputed suppliers and are subjected to close scrutiny, with a one-stop rejection policy to ensure material compliance. Inspections are carried out at various process stages to ensure that our quality standards are maintained. We also have an active development program, allowing us to address special customer requirements, especially for equipment operating in hazardous and corrosive environments.



    Our unit is located in the NEAR M.I.D.C, DOMBIVLI, close to major highways and related industries reducing delivery and dispatch problems. Companies like L&T, RCF, VOLTAS LTD, DOSID ION EXHANGE, ION EXCHANGE, CAPITAL CONTROL, DRIPLEX WATER ENGG,GUJARAT STATE ELECTRICITY BOARD, NTPC TALCHER THERMAL POWER STATION, NTPC SUPER THERMAL POWER STATION, NTPC-SAIL POWER CO.PVT LTD, ANPARA THERMAL POWER STATION ETC, are our major clients.



    We welcome your interest in our products and hope to be service to you. For any further information you may require, please feel free to contact us directly.
